FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YVITY.

How is YVITY Score calculated?
YVITY Score is calculated out of 100 based on Identity (30 pts), Visibility (30 pts) and Credibility (40 pts). Each verified action adds to your score.
How long does IRDAI verification take?
IRDAI license verification typically takes 24–48 hours. Once verified, the License Verified badge appears on your public profile.
What can I do on the Free plan?
Free plan includes basic profile, up to 3 achievements and 5 text testimonials. Upgrade anytime to unlock more features.
Who can see my YVITY profile?
Your public profile is visible to anyone with your profile link. You can control which sections are public or private from My Profile settings.
How do I collect testimonials from clients?
Share your YVITY profile link with clients. They can submit text, audio or video testimonials. All require OTP verification for authenticity.
How does QR code work?
Gold plan advisors get a unique QR code linking to their profile. Print on visiting cards or share digitally — clients scan and land on your verified profile.
What is the Founding Advisor badge?
Founding Advisor badge is given to the first 500 Gold plan members. A special recognition for being among the early believers in credibility-first advisory.
Can I upgrade from Free to Gold directly?
Yes! You can upgrade directly from Free to Silver or Gold anytime from your dashboard via UPI, card or net banking.
How do I know if an advisor is genuine?
Look for the Identity Verified and License Verified badges. Check their YVITY Score — higher score means more verified credibility.
Do I need to register to find an advisor?
No registration needed to search and browse advisors. You only need a free account to give a testimonial or recommend an advisor.
What does YVITY Verified mean?
YVITY Verified means the advisor's identity, IRDAI license and contact details have been verified by our team. Testimonials are OTP-verified by real clients.
Is my personal data safe on YVITY?
Yes. YVITY uses industry-standard encryption. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.
